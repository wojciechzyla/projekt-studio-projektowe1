As a family traveler, we always prioritize amusement parks on our list of places to visit. It's a great way to have fun and spend quality time with our family. We also love to try local cuisine as it gives us a taste of the local culture. Renting a car is a must-have for us as it provides us with the flexibility to move around at our pace. It is a great way to connect with the local Jewish community and learn about their customs and traditions.
